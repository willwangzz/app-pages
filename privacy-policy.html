PhonetiKit 正音

Privacy Policy

隐私政策

Overview

PhonetiKit 正音 is designed with your privacy as a core principle. The app helps you manage phonetic names for your contacts — and it does so entirely on your device.

PhonetiKit does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Contacts Access

PhonetiKit requests access to your Contacts in order to read and update phonetic name fields. This access is:

  • Local only — contact data is read and written directly on your device
  • Never transmitted — no contact information is sent to any server
  • User-controlled — you can revoke access at any time in System Settings

Data Collection

PhonetiKit collects no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No tracking or advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs or services
  • No network requests are made by the app

Data Storage

All data processed by PhonetiKit resides in your device's local Contacts database, managed by the operating system. PhonetiKit does not maintain a separate database or store copies of your contacts.

Children's Privacy

PhonetiKit does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Since the app collects no data, changes are expected to be minimal.

概述

PhonetiKit 正音以保护您的隐私为核心原则。本应用帮助您管理联系人的注音信息，所有操作均在您的设备上完成。

PhonetiKit 不收集、不传输、不共享任何个人数据。一切信息均保留在您的设备上。

通讯录访问

PhonetiKit 需要访问您的通讯录以读取和更新联系人的注音字段。此访问权限：

  • 仅限本地 — 联系人数据仅在您的设备上读取和写入
  • 绝不传输 — 任何联系人信息均不会发送到任何服务器
  • 用户可控 — 您可以随时在系统设置中撤销访问权限

数据收集

PhonetiKit 不收集任何数据，具体包括：

  • 不收集任何个人信息
  • 无使用分析或遥测
  • 无跟踪或广告标识符
  • 无第三方 SDK 或服务
  • 应用不发起任何网络请求

数据存储

PhonetiKit 处理的所有数据均存储在您设备本地的通讯录数据库中，由操作系统管理。PhonetiKit 不维护单独的数据库，也不存储联系人的副本。

儿童隐私

PhonetiKit 不收集任何人的数据，包括 13 岁以下儿童。本应用适合所有年龄段的用户使用。

政策变更

如本隐私政策有更新，修改内容将在此页面发布并注明更新日期。由于本应用不收集任何数据，预计变更将非常少。
