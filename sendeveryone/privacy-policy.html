Privacy Policy

SendEveryone

Overview

SendEveryone respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Contacts Access

SendEveryone requests access to your contacts solely to let you select recipients for group messages and emails. Your contacts data:

  • Is accessed only on your device
  • Is never uploaded or sent to any server
  • Is not stored by the app beyond the current session

Message Sending

Messages and emails are sent through your device's native system APIs (Message Framework, Mail). SendEveryone does not intercept, read, or store the content of any messages you send.

Data Collection

SendEveryone does not collect any data. There are no analytics, tracking, or third-party services.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at willwangzz@outlook.com.

概述

SendEveryone 尊重您的隐私。本应用不会收集、存储或向外部服务器传输任何个人数据。

通讯录访问

SendEveryone 请求访问您的通讯录，仅用于选择群发消息和邮件的收件人。您的通讯录数据：

  • 仅在您的设备上访问
  • 绝不会上传或发送至任何服务器
  • 应用不会在当前会话之外存储这些数据

消息发送

消息和邮件通过设备原生系统 API（Message Framework、Mail）发送。SendEveryone 不会拦截、读取或存储您发送的任何消息内容。

数据收集

SendEveryone 不收集任何数据。没有分析、追踪或第三方服务。

第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方服务或 SDK。

联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系 willwangzz@outlook.com。
